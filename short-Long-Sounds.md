## Short Vowels
```
A - 'ah'  as in 'apple'  - hat
E - 'eh'  as in 'eggs'   - peg
I - 'ih'  as in 'insect' - ping
O - 'awe' as in 'bog'    - got
U - 'uh'  as in 'tug'    - yum 
```

## Long Vowel rules - Sounds the name of the letter
```
A - Ai - ah 
E - ee - ah 
I - I  - ee
O - O  - aa
U - U  - aa
```
